Hello! I am currently working as a post-doc in the Biorobotics Lab at the École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland under the NCCR Robotics grant.

I am currently leading the hardware development effort for one of our amphibious field robots within the Biorobotics Lab at EPFL. Alongside my own research, I supervise Master student projects on the hardware and a couple collaborations with other institutes within our program.

I completed my PhD with the Biomimetics and Dexterous Manipulation Lab at Stanford University. I worked on perching, grasping and multimodal robots. My work focused on applying controllable adhesives to enable robotic systems, such as small aerial robots.
